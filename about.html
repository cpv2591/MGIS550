About Comtrack

Vision Statement

ComTrack is designed to be usable and accessible for people who want to start investing but have no idea or little idea where to start. Overall, many investors feel the need to closely monitor their stocks or cryptos, leading to untimely trades and people disenfranchised with the market. Through our website, new traders will not feel this urge, and become more financially savvy. Our goal is to cater towards new investors who are interested in tracking cryptocurrency. Users will not need to be financially adept to fully utilize our application.

Stocks

What are Stocks?

Stocks are what give shareholders part of ownership in a company. Many companies issue stock in order to pay off debts, launch new products, and many other reasons. There are common stocks and preferred stocks to which in a common stock, owners can vote at shareholder meetings and receive dividends while in a preferred stock, stockholders usually do not have voting rights, but they receive dividend payments before common stockholders do.

What are the Advantages of Investing in Stocks?

  • Potential for growth in stock over a long period of time.
  • Easy to buy.
  • Easy to sell.

What are the Disdvantages of Investing in Stocks?

  • Risk: Fluctuation in stock prices.
  • Research: time consuming.
  • Emotional effects from fluctuation in stock prices.
  • Professional Competition: Hired investors vs. Being a personal investor.
